Sarmale recipe

A delicious traditional recipe for making the most incredible Romanian cabbage rolls (sarmale). They are "sarmale de post" which translates to vegan sarmale

All you need is:

  • A romanian companion
  • Tuica (a traditional alchool romanian drink)
  • aaand .. friends to share the sarmale with!!! Everything tastes better when you share it with your friends and family!
